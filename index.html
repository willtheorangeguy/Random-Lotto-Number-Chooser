Random Lotto Number Chooser

This random lotto number chooser will automatically pick 6 random numbers for you, and they will rarely be the same! Then you can reset in and generate another random 6 numbers.


Star Watch Fork Issue Download

Features

  • Generates 6 random numbers
  • The 6 numbers are rarely the same
  • Allows you to reset the numbers

Purpose

The purpose of this program was to be a test for working in Python, but is now a great way for anyone looking to find 6 or any number of lucky numbers when they need it from their desktop computer.

Use Instructions

  • Click the 'Get My Lucky Numbers' button in the bottom left hand corner of the app.

  • The app will then generate 6 random numbers to replace the '...' in the top 6 squares.

  • Click the reset button in the bottom right corer to get new numbers.
